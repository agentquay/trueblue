HOW

We Grow.

WHAT DIFFERENTIATES US FROM OTHER GROWERS?

In order to be successful in today’s ever-changing markets, we believe it is imperative that farmers be progressive in education, farming techniques, farm management, and marketing. We believe that integrated pest management practices, good agricultural practices, promoting beneficial insects, encouraging clean, cultivated fields, reducing the amounts of chemical usage, and allowing “Mother Nature’s” help in our fields are the keys to our farming success.

Food Safety

  • True Blue’s Growing and Harvesting Practices

    – Our advanced technologies provide easy product traceability.

  • Michigan Agriculture Environment Assurance Program Certified (MAEAP)

    – A voluntary program to prevent or minimize agricultural pollution risks.

  • Agriculture Compliance

    – True Blue Farms operates within all State and Federal agricultural and food safety regulations and requirements.

  • Third Party Audits and Certificates

    – AIB, Primus GFSI, USDA, Kosher, and an Organized HAACP Plan.

Process & Packing

At True Blue Farms, we have a very extensive frozen processing and fresh packing plant operation. Since we receive our fresh picked blueberries directly from our fields, it allows us to cool, pack, sell and ship to the buyers quickly and efficiently, while ensuring maximum freshness to all our blueberry customers.

The True Blue Advantage

  • We grow our blueberries on our own farms, for nearly 75 years and four generations.
  • We pack our blueberries in our packing facility with exceptionally high quality standards.
  • We market our blueberries through our True Blue Berry Management Company.
  • There is No “Middle-Man” or Brokers - you deal directly with the grower.
  • We are customer and consumer satisfaction driven, offering competitive prices, quality, service and loyalty.
